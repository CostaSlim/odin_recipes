Pizza Diavola Recipe

Description

Pizza Diavola means “devil pizza”. It’s named that because it’s spicy. Spicy salami and hot chili peppers make this one hell of a pizza!

Ingredients

  • One pizza dough ball
  • Pizza Sauce
  • 80-100g fresh mozzarella
  • 5-10 slices of spicy salama
  • One thinly sliced chili peeper
  • A handful of black olives

Steps

  1. Stretch out the pizza dough.
  2. Spread the sauce evenly throughout the pizza base using a spoon starting from the center of the pizza. Make sure to leave half an inch at the edge of the pizza without sauce to create a nice edge (“cornicione”) that will puff up in the oven.
  3. Sprinkle over the mozzarella.
  4. Add spicy salami, chilies, and olives.
  5. Bake the pizza for 60-90 seconds at 850°F (450°C). Alternatively, bake on a preheated pizza steel in your home oven for 4-8 minutes at the hottest setting.
